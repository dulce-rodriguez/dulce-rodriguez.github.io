PURPOSE

Our purpose is to provide support to all businesses from Guatemala at all levels. We do this with the purpose of helping the economy of the Country to increase, by promoting these businesses both nationally and internationally. If you are getting started with your business, or have been in the business for years, this website you will help you find all the resources necessary to grow and promote what you do across the Country.
