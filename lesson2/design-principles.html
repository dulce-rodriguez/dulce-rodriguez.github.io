Design Principles

Dulce A. Rodriguez

Alignment

Pick up Limes

pickuplimes.com

Pick up Limes is the best website I found to describe Alignment. As you can see, the different recipes offered here are completely aligned both side by side and one on top of the other. Another thing that I noticed, is that they did a really good job by using a responsive website, since the smalled I made the screen, the more the images changed, but always keeping them aligned. Everything is completely aligned to these images.

Repetition

Downshiftology

downshiftology.com

Downshiftology used repetition in this website specifically with the font. As you can see both on the menu, and on the title, and on the different text boxes throughout the website, she is using the same font which makes it look profesional, and refined. It would be quite messy if she had all different types of fonts, but she kept it simple and achieved a lot by doing so.

Contrast

Yoga With Adriene

yogawithadriene.com

yogawithadriene has most of the website with a white background. Many of the images have a soft background color, and this is the only image she used with a bold black color. You can see how it contrasts really good with the rest of the website, and how it works so good. There aren't a lot of colors in this website, but she did used a couple of them to contrast specially her photos and content which is the most important part of her website.
