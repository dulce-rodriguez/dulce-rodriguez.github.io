Target Audience

The target audience for this website, is for young adults between the ages of 21-30 years old, who like to have an active life apart from their jobs and studies. They have full-time jobs, a college degree, and have an income of around $55,000/year.

Persona one - Blair

Blair is a 22 year old who is still in college but already has a full-time job in a prestigious Fashion company. She loves her job, but is also very concerned about her social life and development.

Scenario 1

Blair is constantly being invited to important work events where there will be a lot of opportunities for her to introduce herself with important designers, so she needs to stay updated in what will be the best outfits to wear to match the weather and to be taken seriously.


Solution

With the up to date news of the weather, Blair will be able to know wether she needs to bring a jacket, or leave it at home for her important events.

Scenario 2

Blair has found that going hiking every Saturday morning has helped her to keep doing what she does without getting too stressed out. It is essencial for her to do this every week as it is the only time that she has to dedicate to herself.


Solution

We provide with a preview of what the weather will be like throughout the week so she knows if it will be rainy, or if it is going to be possible for her to go hiking, or if she needs to find a different activity an plan ahead for it during the week.

Persona two - Charlie

Charlie is a 25 year old husband who just started his career as an accountant in an important company. He is very pationate about being on time, and about always giving his best in all he does. It is very important for him to be on time even if he lives far away from town.

Scenario 1

It's day one, and it's been raining all week, he is not sure if he needs to leave early to get there on time, or if he needs to consider a different route.


Solution

With our constant updates and news about the weather, Charlie will be able to know if frafic is going to be heavie due to the weather, or if he can have some extra minutes to sleep before heading to work.

Scenario 2

Charlie's wife has asked him to invite his friends over to have fun during the weekend, and to share a meal together in their new yard. Charlie's wife is allergic to the sun, so he needs to make sure that everything will be good for them to have a good time.


Solution

Any expected changes of weather are posted in our important updates section, so Charlie knows if they can take the party outsite, or if they'll need to keep it inside to protect his wife.
